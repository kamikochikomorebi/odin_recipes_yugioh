How To Build A Yu-Gi-Oh Deck For Beginners

Overall Deck Building Basics

A Yu-Gi-Oh deck must have at least 40 cards but there is no upper limit. However, keeping closer to 40 of your absolute best cards gives you a higher chance of drawing an extremely powerful card. Be careful though, if a player is unable to draw a card from the deck at the beginning of their turn, that player automatically loses the duel. It is generally recommended for beginners to have a mix of about 20 monsters, 10 spells, and 10 traps but you can alter these amounts as you get a feel for the game. Control-oriented decks will typically include more traps for disruption, while combo-based decks may have more monsters and spells to execute their combinations. It's best to include a few of each of the different sub-types of traps, spells, and monsters in your deck and dueling a few times to see the pros and cons of each before altering the golden ration.
